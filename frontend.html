FRONT-END DEVELOPER- Two Years Diploma

One million computer programming jobs are estimated to be created yearly across the globe - thanks to unprecedented growth in the tech sector. Here at Remote Academy we pinpoint the key skills needed for the most in-demand jobs and develop courses and diplomas to train our students in those areas. That’s why we've developed this two year diploma in front-end web development, which you can complete entirely online with the help of a dedicated counsellor.

The front-end developer is a fundamental part of any web team, working closely with back-end developers. They are often in charge of:
  • Bringing mock-ups to life in a beautiful and consistent manner
  • Communicating with back-end developers for information exchange across the application
  • Using best practices to structure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code as components
  • Writing tests to ensure the site’s front-end accounts for all possible uses
  • Ensuring the maintenance and growth of a site’s front-end
This program is accessible to all, but the following recommendations would be helpful:
  • A high school diploma / GCSE/WASSCE level
  • A good level of English is recommended
  • Access to a computer (PC or Mac), headphones, a webcam
  • Good internet access
Upon completion of your studies and validation of your skills and project by an academic panel, you will earn a government approved "Web developer diploma” equivalent to an Ordinary National Diploma (OND)
