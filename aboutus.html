WHO WE ARE

We are a non-profit organization that focuses on technology and computing as a way of reaching out and teaching the less privileged and disabled children. The company aims to encourage the development of innovative approach to educate these children in computing and information technology by bringing low cost technological equipment to public schools, IDP camps and poor regions. This is both an economic and social development that can help shape the future of these children. Furthermore, there are adaptive and assistive technology tailored for children with special and physical needs such as the Braille keyboard, Intel readers, sip and puff system, graphic organizers, text-to-speech and so on. As technology revamps, the socio-economic effects improve the lives of impoverished people in Nigeria, we believe that disadvantaged children in our society should not be left out, especially if they can be aided through technology.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to bridge the educational gap between the average Nigerian child and those who are less privileged, disabled, without access to schools or in IDP camps through the provision of technological equipment with access to educational materials and also teaching them through technology.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to engage less privileged and disabled children of age 8-17 years, thereby giving them sound knowledge of information technology at an early stage.

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring less privileged children have equal opportunites educationally in our society.
