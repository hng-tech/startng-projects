Our Mission


To ensure the prosperity of the Amazon Rainforest Forest and its plant and animal species by engaging relevant stakeholders, developing expertise in forestry practice and using technology to create practical solutions to maximise the benefit for dependants on the Amazon Rain Forest and the global climate ecosystem.

Our Vision


We envision a world in which forests are thriving and valued for their significant environmental, societal and economic benefits.

Objectives


  1. Conserve Nature

    2. We help in the conservation of nature by putting in place protective measures, playing our part in aforestation and enriching the Amazon as well as encouraging the growth of its species.

  2. Reduce Pressing Threats

    3. We concentrate on reducing threats to the Amazon by e ducating people on protective and preventive measures to be taken.

  3. Protect

    4. We protect the Amazon by providing aid through your donations.

Five Year Projections


we will plant around 60 million trees in forest restoration projects in all 50 states. We will also work in dozens of cities across South America, expanding tree canopy and improving the quality of life for residents.


Together, these projects will recover hundreds of thousands of acres of wildlife habitat, safeguard vital watersheds, absorb millions of tons of greenhouse gases and protect some of the most stunning landscapes in America. At the same time, we will be using trees and greenspace to make our communities more sustainable, beautiful and livable.
