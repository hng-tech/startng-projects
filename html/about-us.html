About NairoCare Foundation

We are changing millions of lives through technology and Charity.

Founded in 2019, Nairocare is a social enterprise that applies technology and innovative tools to raise funds and resources for Internally Displaced Persons(IDPs) in Nigeria. A pioneer in the field of social advocacy married with the use of financial technology tools. Nairocare uses an array of technology tools to simplify fundraising and crowdfunding for the Internally Displaced Persons(IDP) across Nigeria. We are in constant partnership with civil societies, NGOs, CSOs, public institutions and the media, chiefly in the areas of advocacy, community engagement, research, innovation, and technology. Nairocare's methodology is deploying refined skill sets to creatively develop tools and platforms to strategically crowdfund and raise funds for the education, shelter, health, and well-being of every Internally Displaced Persons(IDP).

NairoCare Foundation is a community of like minded young Nigerians passionate about creating positive change in the country and particularly dedicated to help relieve the harsh conditions faced by people living in IDP camps and help accelerate their re-integration back into society. Our mission was born out of the burning desire to solve the biggest problems in the Nigerian society. We intend to leverage the use of technology and humanitarian efforts to alleviate the pain and suffering of internally displaced persons.

Our Mission

To raise funds and resources for the sole aim of reviving the hopes and dreams of Internally Displaced Persons in Nigeria and making their re-integration into society a seamless one.

Our Work

We help various IDPs access qualitative health care
We partner with the industry professionals to help train the IDPs on current and employable skills which will help them get a decent job in Nigeria which would help ease their re-intergration process
We follow them up by helping them to access funds to start a small scale businesses.
>We also ensure that out-of-school children receive education by helping to pay for school tuition, levies and basic school supplies through donation and direct grants.
We appreciate partners and volunteers who are ready to drive this agenda

How do we stand out?

We are 100% transparent

We, at NairoCare realize most people have a problem donating to charity organizations because they believe the funds are not properly allocated. That is why we go the extra mile to be as transparent as possible. Our ledger is available on our website for anyone to access.
Our ledger shows; how much we receive, how much we spend, what we spend on and how much we have left.
