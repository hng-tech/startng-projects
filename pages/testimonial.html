Testimonials

Kintu Balarabe, Pupil

Auta Community, Togo.


“When I was in primary 2, I used to sit on the floor in class. Whenever it rained, because of the leaking roof, the floor got wet. So, I’ll gather tree branches to sit on for class on such days. Since these people came to fix everything, I sit on chair and table and the roof does not leak. I am always very happy to go to school now.”

Duza Yolanda - Student

Shendam community, Tanzania.


"In my school, almost every morning there were fights over chairs and desks in my class that ended up very bad injuries. We are 73 students with only 10 chairs and desks. Those who didn't get chairs sat on the floor or windows. But now, for the past one month, there has been no fight because everyone has the same chairs and desks. We even have a new white board which helps people at the back of the class see better."

Maria Stone.

StartNG Official


In July 2018, I donated to MSF. At the end of the month, I received an email update on what my donation had been used for. The report showed the number of chairs and desks that my donation funded and how many children benefited. I have been a recurring donor ever since as I am impressed with everything about MSF.”

Lilongwe Obasanjo

Head of Firdaus Community School.


"Looking back to when we had classrooms with no chairs, it was not easy teaching the children. When I want to scold a child for not writing properly, I see the child on their belly and I can't blame s/he. Posture affects writing. But now, my class kids are so happy learning, and their writing is unbelievable above my expectations because of chairs and desks at their school. I can walk through their seats to monitor progress. Thank you MSF"

Minds & Souls Foundation is an organization dedicated to eradicating illiteracy in Africa - one child, one community and one country at a time.
